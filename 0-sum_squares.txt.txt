This program will calculate the sum of squares of a given numbers.
1.	take input form the users 
2.	assign the numbers to a variable, (x=first number, y=second number)
3.	multiply the number by itself and their products and assign it to new variable called sum (sum=x*x + y*y)
4.	display the sum of their respective squares (the sum of squares of x and y is sum)
